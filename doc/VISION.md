# ClawCompany — Vision & Brand

> **Build for OPC. Every human being is a chairman.**
> Your Claws company, one key to run them all.

---

## 1. The OPC model (One Person Company)

ClawCompany is infrastructure for OPC — One Person Companies powered by AI. Every human is a Chairman. Your AI team executes autonomously.

| Traditional company | ClawCompany OPC |
|---|---|
| Hire 10 people, manage them daily | One command, AI team runs |
| $50K/month payroll | $0.02-0.05 per mission |
| Weeks to deliver | Minutes to deliver |
| You do the work | You set the goal |

**The Chairman does exactly three things:** set goals, approve decisions, review results.

---

## 2. Naming philosophy

```
Claw (singular)  = OpenClaw — one claw, one agent, working alone.
Claws (plural)   = ClawCompany — a pack of claws, autonomous collaboration.
Company          = Not a tool. A company with departments, hierarchy, and budgets.
```

## 5. Ecosystem

```
ClawCompany  — Autonomous company layer (goal in → result out)
ClawAPI      — Model supply layer (8 models, 1 key, crypto-native)
OpenClaw     — Agent tools layer (single-agent execution)
LLMs         — Raw inference (Opus, Sonnet, GPT, Gemini, OSS)
```

ClawCompany is the top layer. It combines everything below into an autonomous company.
